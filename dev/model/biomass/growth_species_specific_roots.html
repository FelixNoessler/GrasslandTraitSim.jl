Species-specific growth adjustment - Investments into roots ​

The root investment factor ROOTtxys [-], which reduces growth, is described by:

ROOTtxys=ROOTRSA,txys⋅ROOTAMC,txysROOTRSA,txys=1−κRSA+κRSA⋅exp⁡(log⁡(0.5)ϕRSA⋅TRSAtxys)ROOTAMC,txys=1−κAMC+κAMC⋅exp⁡(log⁡(0.5)ϕAMC⋅TAMCtxys)TRSAtxys=BB,txysBtxys⋅RSAsTAMCtxys=BB,txysBtxys⋅AMCs

  • ϕRSA reference trait value [m² g⁻¹]

  • ϕAMC reference trait value [-]

  • κRSA maximum reduction in growth [-]

  • κAMC maximum reduction in growth [-]

see also SimulationParameter
