Senescence ​

The loss of biomass due to senescence Stxys [kg ha⁻¹] is defined as follows:

Stxys=(1−(1−αSEN)1/30.44)⋅SENtxy⋅(SLAsϕSLA)βSEN,SLA⋅BtxysSENtxy={1ifSTtxy<Ψ11+(SENmax−1)STtxy−Ψ1Ψ2−Ψ1ifΨ1<STtxy<Ψ2SENmaxifSTtxy>Ψ2STtxy=∑i=tmod365tmax(0°C,Tixy)

The monthly senescence rate defined by αSEN is converted to a daily senescence rate in the first equation.

  • αSEN senescence rater per month [-]

  • ϕSLA reference value for the trait specific leaf area [m² g⁻¹]

  • βSEN,SLA scales the influences of the specific leaf area on the senescence rate [-]

  • Ψ1 when the senescence starts to increase [°C]

  • Ψ2 when the senescence reaches the maximum senescence rate [°C]

  • SENmax maximum senescence rate [-]

see also SimulationParameter
