Plant biomass dynamics ​

Click on a process to view detailed documentation:

The change in the biomass B from day t to t+1 of species s in patch x,y [kg ha⁻¹] is calculated based on the actual growth Gact,txys [kg ha⁻¹], and the losses by senescence Stxys [kg ha⁻¹] and management Mtxys [kg ha⁻¹]:

Bt+1xys=Btxys+Gact,txys−Stxys−Mtxys
