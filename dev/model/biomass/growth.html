Growth ​

Click on a process to view detailed documentation:

The actual growth Gact,txys [kg ha⁻¹] is derived from the community potential growth Gpot,txy [kg ha⁻¹] and the multiplicative effect of five growth adjustment factors:

Gact,txys=Gpot,txy⋅LIGtxys⋅NUTtxys⋅WATtxys⋅ROOTtxys⋅ENVtxy

where LIGtxys [-] is the species-specific competition for light, NUTtxys [-] is the species-specific competition for nutrients, WATtxys [-] is the species-specific competition for soil water, ROOTtxys [-] is the species-specific cost for maintaining roots and mycorrhiza, and ENVtxy [-] is the non-species specific adjustment based on environmental and seasonal factors.
