GrasslandTraitSim.grazing! Function
julia
grazing!(; container, LD, above_biomass, actual_height)
\begin{align} \rho &= \left(\frac{LNCM}{LNCM_{cwm]}}\right) ^ {\text{β_PAL_lnc}} \\ μₘₐₓ &= κ \cdot \text{LD} \\ h &= \frac{1}{μₘₐₓ} \\ a &= \frac{1}{\text{α_GRZ}^2 \cdot h} \\ \text{totgraz} &= \frac{a \cdot (\sum \text{biomass})^2} {1 + a\cdot h\cdot (\sum \text{biomass})^2} \\ \text{share} &= \frac{ \rho \cdot \text{biomass}} {\sum \left[ \rho \cdot \text{biomass} \right]} \\ \text{graz} &= \text{share} \cdot \text{totgraz} \end{align}

  • LD daily livestock density [livestock units ha⁻¹]

  • κ daily consumption of one livestock unit [kg], follows [5]

  • ρ palatability, dependent on nitrogen per leaf mass (LNCM) [-]

  • α_GRZ is the half-saturation constant [kg ha⁻¹]

  • equation partly based on [6]
