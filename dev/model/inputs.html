Model inputs ​

Here you find all inputs needed to start a simulation. You can click on the links to the methods to see how the input is used in those methods. If you want to prepare your own inputs, go to the tutorial.

Daily abiotic conditions ​

SymbolDescriptionused in
TtxyTemperature [°C]temperature_reduction!
STtxyYearly cumulative temperature [°C]seasonal_reduction!, seasonal_component_senescence
PtxyPrecipitation [mm d⁻¹]change_water_reserve
PARtxyPhotosynthetically active radiation [MJ ha⁻¹ d⁻¹]potential_growth!, radiation_reduction!
PETtxyPotential evapotranspiration [mm d⁻¹]water_reduction!, evaporation, transpiration

Daily management variables ​

SymbolDescriptionused in
CUTtxyHeight of mowing event, NaN means no mowing [m]mowing!
LDtxyGrazing intensity measured in livestock units, NaN means no grazing [LD ha⁻¹]grazing!

SymbolDescriptionused in

SLAsSpecific leaf area [m² g⁻¹]water_reduction!, calculate_LAI!, senescence!
PHsPlant height [m]potential_growth!, light_competition!, mowing!
RSAsRoot surface area / aboveground biomass [m² g⁻¹]nutrient_competition!, water_reduction!, nutrient_reduction!
ABPsAboveground biomass / total biomass [-]calculate_LAI!
LBPsLeaf mass / total biomass [-]calculate_LAI!
LNCsLeaf nitrogen content per leaf mass [mg g⁻¹]grazing!
